Estimate

An estimate (of something) a guess or judgement about the size, cost, etc. of something, before you have all the facts and figures.

Building Permission

An official certificate of permission issued by local authorities to a builder to construct, enlarge, or alter a building.

Land Mapping

Land cover maps are tools that provide vital information on the Earth's land use and cover patterns. They aide policy development, urban planning, forest and agricultural monitoring etc.

Building Construction

Construction is a general term meaning the art and science to form objects, systems, or organizations, and comes from Latin constructio and Old French construction. To construct is the verb: the act of building, and the noun is construction: how something is built, the nature of its structure.

Interior Designing

Interior design is the science of understanding behaviors to help property owners create a functional room within a building, including the shapes of a room's walls, floors, and more.

Surveying

Surveying or land surveying is the technique, profession, art, and science of determining the terrestrial two-dimensional or three-dimensional positions of points and the distances and angles between them. A land surveying professional is called a land surveyor.

Valuation

Valuation of building or property is the method of calculating the present marketable cost of a building. Valuation of a building depends on the sort of building, its structure, durability, location, size, shape, the width of roads, frontage, types and quality of building materials used and the cost of these materials.

3D Elevation

A 3D elevation is a three-dimensional representation of a two-dimensional elevation found in an architectural drawing. Real estate professionals improve the 3D designs and showcase the photorealistic renderings to their potential buyers.

Site Supervision

Site supervisors monitor the progress of construction projects and ensure compliance with construction safety regulations. They supervise construction workers and subcontractors, educate construction workers on site safety practices, and evaluate employee performance.
